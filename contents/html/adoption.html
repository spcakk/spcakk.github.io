Adopt a Shelter Animal and Save a Life.

🐶 Adoption Procedures 🐱

  1. Visit our shelter at Kg Gana Papar.
  2. Find, fall in love, and bound with an animal or more.
  3. Fill in the adoption form on the animal you wish to open your loving home to.
  4. Schedule a house visit with our Adoption Team.
    A house visit is mandatory for us to ensure that safety and living conditions of our beloved animals are met before the animal arrives.
  5. An adoption fee will be collected to confirm your booking once the house visit is complete.
  6. Notify SPCAKK that the preparations are done.
  7. After approval, SPCAKK will send the animal to the adopters house ASAP.
  8. Welcome your fury friends to their new home. 💗
