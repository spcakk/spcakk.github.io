Adoption Procedures

  1. Visit our shelter Kg Gana Papar
  2. Find and bound with an animal
  3. After choosing the animal of your interest. Fill in the adoption form
  4. Schedule a house visit (House visit is mandatory so SPCAKK team can ensure the safety and living conditions of our beloved animals)
  5. SPCAKK team will conduct a house visit and advise where/what needs to be add/improve
  6. At the end of the house visit, adoption fee will be collected to confirm the animal booking
  7. Adopter will be given plenty of time to fullfill what is asked
  8. Notify SPCAKK that the preparations are done
  9. After approval, SPCAKK will send the animal to the adopters house ASAP
  10. Welcome your fury friends to their new home 💗
