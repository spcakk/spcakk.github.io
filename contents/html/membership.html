Becoming a Member

Fill in an application form at our animal adoption center or any SPCAKK events, such as pet adoption drive and charity bazaar. Or you may call us on 019-8611732/016 8805400 for over-the-phone application. You will be notified once your application has been approved by the committee.

There are 3 types of membership:

Type Price
Ordinary Membership RM50 per year
Life Membership RM500 (one-off payment)
Junior Membership (below 17 years old) RM10 per year
Corporate Membership RM1,000 per year

The committee reserves the right to decline an application or renewal of membership.
